002 : F22 Raptor Scale Model


"The engine is the heart of an airplane, but the pilot is its soul." — Walter Raleigh



I built an F-22 Raptor model using a custom laser-cut frame. It’s powered by a 1950KV 2207 brushless motor paired with a 40A ESC. I’m currently running it on a 6S battery for high performance. The plane uses ELRS for control, along with two servos connected through pushrods for the control surfaces. I’m considering upgrading the ESC for even better performance.
